Our Story

Founded in 2023, FinancialTools.com emerged from a simple idea: to make complex financial calculations accessible to everyone. Our team of finance professionals and tech enthusiasts noticed that many people struggle with basic financial planning due to the complexity of calculations involved.

We set out to create a comprehensive suite of tools that would demystify financial planning. Today, we serve over 500,000 users monthly, helping them make informed decisions about investments, loans, taxes, and retirement planning.
